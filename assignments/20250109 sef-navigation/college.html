Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university in Phagwara, Punjab, India. It was established in the Lovely International Trust. The university was created as a tribute to the late Sh. Baldev Raj Mittal, the founder of the Lovely Group. How it started The Lovely Group, which was successful in the sweets and automobile dealership businesses, wanted to give back to society. In 2001, they established their first institution on 3.5 acres of land in Phagwara, Punjab. The college became a deemed university four years later. LPU was the first university in Punjab to apply for the status of a private university.

LPU provides placement opportunities for students across all UG/PG/Diploma programs under various disciplines including Computer Science, Computer Applications, Electronics & Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Polytechnic, Bioengineering & Biosciences, Agriculture, Physical Sciences, Pharmacy & Allied Medical Sciences, Management, Hotel Management & Tourism, Architecture & Planning, Interior & Product Design, Fashion, Multimedia, Journalism, Film and Theatre, Fine Arts, Social Sciences & Languages, Education, Physical Education and Law. The university's Division of Career Services plays a vital role in connecting students with top recruiters across various industries, offering dedicated support to help them secure rewarding career opportunities while completion of their studies.
